dragon noodles

  • prep time: 5 mins
  • cook time: 10 mins
  • serves: 2

ingredients

  • 4 oz. lo mein noodles
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1/4 tsp crushed red pepper
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sriracha (rooster sauce)
  • 1 handful fresh cilantro
  • 1 sliced green onion

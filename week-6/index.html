dragon noodles

  • prep time: 5 mins
  • cook time: 10 mins
  • serves: 2

ingredients

  • 4 oz. lo mein noodles
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1/4 tsp crushed red pepper
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sriracha (rooster sauce)
  • 1 handful fresh cilantro
  • 1 sliced green onion

creamy tomato and spinach pasta

  • prep time: 5 mins
  • cook time: 20 mins
  • serves: 4

ingredients

  • 1/2 lb. penne pasta
  • 1 small onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 (15 oz.) can diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp dried basil
  • pinch red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • freshly cracked pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 oz. cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated paremsan
  • 1/2 (9 oz.) bag fresh spinach
